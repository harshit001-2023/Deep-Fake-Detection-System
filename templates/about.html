About DeepFake Detection System

Project Overview

Developed by students at Sipna College of Engineering & Technology, Amravati, this project tackles the escalating threat of DeepFake media. Using cutting-edge AI, our system achieves a 95% accuracy on the Celeb-DF dataset, safeguarding digital media integrity for security and content verification.

Methodology

Our system integrates three core components:

  • MTCNN: Detects and crops faces with precision.
  • MobileNetV2: Extracts spatial features, spotting artifacts like blending issues.
  • LSTM: Analyzes temporal patterns to catch unnatural movements.

Trained on 199 Celeb-DF videos (99 real, 100 fake) with 5 frames per video, we optimized thresholds from 0.5 to 0.18 for subtle DeepFake detection.
